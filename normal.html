Sample Diet Plan for Normal People:

Breakfast:

  • 1 cup of oatmeal with low-fat milk and fresh berries
  • 2 slices of whole-grain toast with 1 tablespoon of almond butter
  • 1 small banana
  • Black coffee or unsweetened green tea

Snack:

  • 1 small apple
  • 10 unsalted almonds
  • Water or unsweetened tea

Lunch:

  • Grilled chicken breast or tofu with mixed green salad (lettuce, spinach, kale) and low-fat dressing
  • 1 small whole-grain pita bread
  • 1 cup of vegetable soup
  • Water or unsweetened tea

Snack:

  • 1 cup of sliced carrots and celery sticks
  • 2 tablespoons of hummus
  • Water or unsweetened tea

Dinner:

  • Grilled fish (salmon, tilapia, or cod) with steamed vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower, or asparagus)
  • 1/2 cup of brown rice or quinoa
  • Water or unsweetened tea

Snack:

  • 1 small pear
  • 10 unsalted pistachios
  • Water or unsweetened tea

Remember to drink at least 8 glasses of water throughout the day.

Please note that this is just a sample diet plan and should be adjusted according to individual needs and preferences. It's always recommended to consult a registered dietitian before starting a new diet.
