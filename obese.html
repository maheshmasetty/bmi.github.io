Diet Plan for Obese People:

Breakfast:

  • 1 cup of low-fat Greek yogurt with fresh berries and 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 2 slices of whole-grain toast with 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1 small apple
  • Black coffee or unsweetened green tea

Snack:

  • 1 hard-boiled egg
  • 1 small orange
  • Water or unsweetened tea

Lunch:

  • Grilled chicken breast or turkey breast with mixed green salad (lettuce, spinach, kale) and low-fat dressing
  • 1 small whole-grain pita bread
  • 1 cup of vegetable soup
  • Water or unsweetened tea

Snack:

  • 1 cup of sliced cucumbers and cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of low-fat hummus
  • Water or unsweetened tea

Dinner:

  • Grilled salmon or tuna with steamed vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower, or asparagus)
  • 1/2 cup of quinoa or wild rice
  • Water or unsweetened tea

Snack:

  • 1 small pear
  • 10 unsalted almonds
  • Water or unsweetened tea

Remember to drink at least 8 glasses of water throughout the day.

Please note that this is just a sample diet plan and should be adjusted according to individual needs and preferences. It's always recommended to consult a registered dietitian before starting a new diet.
